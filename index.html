About Me

Heyo! Thanks for taking the time to visit my site. My name is Terrence Mahnken, residing in Mount Juliet, TN, working in Nashville, TN, and originally from Florida. Currently, I work as a Senior Consultant at the Tennessee Board of Regents (TBR) for Ellucian which provides higher education software solutions. I provide support, consultation, training, and administration for a number of front-end and infrastructure products for TBR and the TBR institutions. This diversity has provided a wide range of opportunities to observe and immerse myself in several facets of IT including networking, Linux administration, identity and access management, security and certificate management, and user experience methodology. My goal is to dive deeper into the development side of IT after completion of the Vanderbilt Coding Boot Camp.

I am extremely blessed to have an amazing and supportive family. My wife of 11 years, Helene, and my 4 year-old son, Everett, are my pride and joy. My two dogs, Hunter and Alfie, provide lots of laughs, craziness, and hair to our home. When I am not busy at work or attending the boot camp classes, I enjoy spending time with said family, reading, and keeping busy with home projects.
